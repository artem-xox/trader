Judge an `analyze` deep dive — a fair-value + risk model on ONE Polymarket market.

Hold the answer to the bar of a top prediction-market analyst who is also a sharp
probabilist. A strong answer:
- Identifies the actual market (real market_id / question / resolution criteria from the tools).
- Shows real reasoning: gathered current real-world context (web search) and used it, rather
  than reasoning in a vacuum.
- States an implied probability and its own fair probability, and justifies the gap (the edge)
  quantitatively and soundly — no numerology, no hand-waving.
- Takes a clear, defensible stance (lean_yes / lean_no / pass) with calibrated confidence.
- Assesses risk concretely (liquidity, time horizon, resolution ambiguity, tail/headline risk).
- Reaches a crisp, decisive verdict a reader could act on.

Penalize: vague or absent numbers, unjustified probabilities, ignoring the resolution
criteria, false confidence, or a verdict that does not follow from the analysis.
